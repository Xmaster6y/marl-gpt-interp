## Topic Fit

The project fits the workshop through agent-based modelling and reinforcement learning, player dynamics, play evaluation, and practical sports analytics. The sports audience makes the modelling gap between simulator agents and human trajectories more compelling than a purely architectural interpretability story.

## Project Relevance

The abstract should emphasize interpretability of football concepts, human tracking data, and the simulator-human modelling gap. It should not overpromise finished MARL-GPT experiments before weights and human data access are available.
